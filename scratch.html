Riftbound – A League of Legends Card Game

A strategic, deckbuilder that brings Runeterra’s champions to life in a whole new way.

What Is Riftbound?

Riftbound is the official physical League of Legends trading card game (TCG) developed by Riot Games and published by UVS Games in English-speaking regions. Set in the world of Runeterra, it allows 2–4 players to compete by controlling shared zones called Battlefields to earn points, rather than reducing an opponent's life total.

How Riftbound Works

  1. Build your deck
    • Select a Legend that fits your playstyle
    • Find the champion unit that matches up with said Legend
    • Add 39 spells and units that match the domain of your Legend (the two colors shown on the Legend card)
    • Add a total of 12 runes using the two domain colors
  2. Gather resources and play cards from your deck
  3. Score points by capturing battlefields and defending them from your enemy
  4. Win by being the first player to hit 8 points

While it features complex strategic depth and keywords like Ganking, Ambush, and Hunt, the game is designed to be accessible to newcomers while offering a unique emotional connection to League of Legends champions through its deck-building requirements.
